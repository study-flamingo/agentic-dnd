## Tips for DMs

1. **Tag all players** in scene posts so they get notifications
2. **Be specific about needed rolls** â€” don't make players guess
3. **Set clear time windows** and stick to them
4. **Use the advance rule** â€” after 24h, move forward with sensible defaults
5. **Roll contested checks yourself** to maintain pacing (goblin's Acrobatics vs player's Athletics)
6. **Keep resolution posts under control** â€” summarize rather than novelize
7. **End with clear next steps** â€” what should players do now?
